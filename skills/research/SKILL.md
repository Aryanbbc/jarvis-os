# Research Skill

## Description
Use this skill when the user asks Jarvis OS to find, search, research, investigate, compare, summarize, or gather information on a topic.

## Trigger Words
- find
- search
- research
- investigate
- look up
- gather
- compare
- summarize
- explore
- report

## Steps
1. Understand the user's research topic clearly.
2. Identify the exact question or goal.
3. Search for relevant information.
4. Collect key findings.
5. Organize the information into sections.
6. Highlight important facts, sources, and conclusions.
7. Save the final research output inside the vault.
8. Update `vault/index.md` with the new report link and word count.

## Output Format

# Research Report: <Topic>

Date: <YYYY-MM-DD>

## Objective
<What the user wanted to find or understand>

## Key Findings
- <Finding 1>
- <Finding 2>
- <Finding 3>

## Detailed Notes
<Full research explanation>

## Sources
- <Source 1>
- <Source 2>

## Summary
<Short final conclusion>

## Next Actions
- <Recommended next step>

## Where To Save In Vault
Save research reports to:

vault/reports/<YYYY-MM-DD>-<topic-slug>.md
